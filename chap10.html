Begin Again: Becoming a Lifelong Diplomacy Player

As is taught in meditation practices around the world, the important thing is to notice, and then begin again.

With each new game within me rises hope, potential for heroism, and the sweet taste of victory.

This same hope rests in each playerâ€™s heart and mind, which makes for an interesting affair, to say the least!

This book is the beginning of my attempt to develop a Philosophy of Diplomacy, and, so equipped, build a roadmap for becoming a better Diplomacy player.

A grander vision, if I may, would be for such a roadmap to align with my efforts to become a better human. (In a game which seems to encourage lies and deceit, it very much remains to be seen if these goals are mutually compatible.)

As I mentioned in the Overture, this book is the beginning of a project which will continue. I hope to endeavor to elaborate more completely my experiences and potential insights, so as to be less wrong in the future. As a fallible being, that is my hope for us all.

You can find me at local Diplomacy games in Minnesota and with any luck at the next Diplomacy tournament near you.
